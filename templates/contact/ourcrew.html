Our Crew

Do you like what you see, and maybe even want to learn more? Come follow us as we adventure across Canada. You can find photos, videos and more at our various Social Media pages.

Our Experience

Our combined years of experience were in customer service and training, ranging from safety awareness to physical fitness. Our skill sets compliment each other, challenging ourselves everyday to eat well and stay in shape, so we can handle the different challenges that arrive while onboard.

Brian Lymburner Captain

Brian has been in and around boats his whole life. Growing up in Port Colbourne he was always around the water. He has his license for boating, safety, radio, and is enthusiastic about retiring on the water and all the adventures that it brings.

Boating License, CPR-C First-Class

Lynn Lymburner First Mate

Lynn has grown up around kayaks and canoes. 5 years ago, she was introduced to power yachts, fell in love and hasn't looked back. Lynn is a certified personal trainer & thai massage therapist. She has obtained her boat & radio licences and looking forward to more certifications in the future.

Licensed Massage Therapist, CPR-C First-Class

Our Favourite Places

Pelee Island, Lake Erie, Canada
The Island is located at the west end of Lake Erie. It has it all, great anchorages and nice beaches with trails. It even has an LCBO.
Erieau, Lake Erie, Canada
What a beautiful part of Ontario. Located between Rondeau Bay and Lake Erie. It has great fishing, a big beach with lots of hiking trails. This small town is full of unique boutiques & restaurants.
