Ready For A Daycation?

Explore Ontario's beautiful lakes, and rivers with us. Leave the troubles of life on the shore and come and get in on the fun and the sun. Participate in one of our pre-built "Daycation" packages. From live music, to dinner on the water watching the sun set. We've got something for everyone.

Daycations
Sunset Cruise

Nothing beats watching the sunset over water! Bring your camera to capture breath-taking moments that you'll never forget. Perfect for date nights or family events.

Drop A Line

Fishing in the wonderful Lakes of Ontario, you never know what you may catch. Dinner anyone?! Grab a fishing pole, cast out and watch the day go by.

Thai Massage

Feeling a little beat down, or maybe a little beat up? Come join us on the beautiful Ontario waters and relax in a way that you've never experienced before. Our certified massage therapist will give you an authentic thai massage in the most serene environment possible, for an event that you have to experience to truly appreciate.

Blues And Cruise
